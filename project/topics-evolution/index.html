Topics Evoluation in Quora

 

Did topics ever change in online Q&A community such as Quora?

We use python to crawl data from Quora in topic of films and televsion, dividing them into sections according to cycle of every episode. With powerful tools such as LDA and BTM to analyse the topics changing over time, it is simple for us to detect and discover the trends of topics evolution in Quora.

Can our algorithm analyzes the topics trend better?

With the combination of data about users behaviour, algorithm can be built to improve BTM, which will show us a better result about topics mining. Afterwards, we made suggestions on the personalized recommendation and advertising of Quora Film and Television and other topics.
